We are doing more than you expect

Dosaka - Taste of South is a south Indian casual dining chain of restaurants which is combining creativity and authenticity of very popular south indian cuisines. we started our journey in february 2019 with an aim to serve authentic south indian delicacies with a blend of fusion dishes, contemporary ambience, sustainibility focused processes and usual accomplishments sum up to make the place where anyone and everyone can hang out.

Our specialities are dosai ghee roast , mysore masala dosa , irulli rava masala dosa , fried idli , thatte idli , jinni dosa , pizza dosa and most popular peri peri cheese dosa.
